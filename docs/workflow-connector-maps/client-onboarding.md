# Client Onboarding Connector Map

## Workflow

Move a new client from intake to kickoff with required docs, tasks, scheduling, and clear ownership.

## Common Mess

Intake data is incomplete, docs are scattered, kickoff steps are inconsistent, and follow-up lives in someone’s inbox.

## Best Connectors

- Form tool or intake page
- Google Drive
- Notion or Airtable
- Google Calendar
- Gmail
- GPT

## Minimum Stack

Forms + Drive + Notion/Airtable + Calendar + GPT.

## Advanced Stack

Add CRM, ClickUp, Slack, or webhooks only after the checklist and review path are stable.

## What To Automate

- Onboarding checklist draft
- Missing-info request draft
- Kickoff agenda draft
- Folder/task setup checklist
- Internal handoff summary

## What Should Stay Human

- Final client commitments
- Scope decisions
- Legal, financial, HR, lending, or compliance interpretation
- Customer-facing sends during pilot

## Risk Level

Medium. High when onboarding includes sensitive financial, legal, HR, or regulated data.

## Draft-First Rules

AI may draft and organize. A human confirms requirements, sends messages, and approves kickoff readiness.

## Starter GPT Prompt

```text
Map this client onboarding workflow into a minimum connector stack. Show intake, docs, tasks, kickoff scheduling, review gates, and a safe 30-day pilot.
```

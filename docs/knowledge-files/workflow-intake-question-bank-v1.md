# Workflow Intake Question Bank

## Purpose
This question bank provides practical, no-BS discovery questions to help audit SMB workflows. Use these questions to uncover the real mess behind manual processes before attempting to automate them.

## How to ask without overwhelming the user
Do not ask 20 questions at once.
- Start with the **3-question quick intake**.
- If they are ready for more detail, use the **5-question standard intake**.
- For full mapping, use the **10-question deep intake**.

---

### 3-Question Quick Intake
1. What process do you want to improve?
2. What is the most painful, manual, or repetitive part of it?
3. What happens if this process goes wrong?

### 5-Question Standard Intake
1. What workflow are we auditing?
2. What tools do you currently use for it?
3. Who owns this process right now?
4. How much time does this cost you per week?
5. Does this touch customers, money, or sensitive data?

### 10-Question Deep Intake
1. What business are you in?
2. What workflow are we auditing?
3. What exact event triggers this workflow?
4. Who owns it?
5. What tools are involved from start to finish?
6. What specific data inputs are required?
7. How often does it happen (volume/frequency)?
8. What breaks most often?
9. What happens if it goes wrong?
10. Does it touch customers, money, sensitive data, legal, HR, tax, accounting, lending, medical, or compliance?

## Questions by Workflow Type

### Lead Management
- **Best opening questions:** How long does it currently take you to respond to a new lead?
- **Process questions:** What happens the moment a lead form is submitted? Where does it go?
- **Data questions:** Do leads provide all the info you need up front, or do you have to chase them?
- **Tool questions:** Which CRM or inbox catches these leads?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if a lead falls through the cracks?
- **Human-review questions:** Do you need to manually qualify leads before sending a calendar link?
- **Success metric questions:** What is your target response time?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do you have clear stages in your pipeline, or is it just a list?

### Sales Follow-up
- **Best opening questions:** How many times do you follow up before giving up?
- **Process questions:** Are follow-ups manual emails, calls, or automated sequences?
- **Data questions:** How do you know when to stop following up?
- **Tool questions:** Do you use a CRM or just your inbox?
- **Risk questions:** Could we annoy prospects by following up too aggressively?
- **Human-review questions:** Should AI draft the follow-up for you to review, or send it automatically?
- **Success metric questions:** How many more deals do we want to close with this?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do you have a consistent message template, or does everyone write their own?

### Quote Follow-up
- **Best opening questions:** How do you track who has received a quote but hasn't signed?
- **Process questions:** What is the trigger to remind a customer about a quote?
- **Data questions:** Is the quote amount and date stored centrally?
- **Tool questions:** Are quotes sent via a specific software (e.g., PandaDoc, QuickBooks)?
- **Risk questions:** Does this touch final pricing or sensitive financial negotiations?
- **Human-review questions:** Do you need to review the reminder to see if you should offer a discount?
- **Success metric questions:** What is your current close rate on quotes?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Are quotes always sent the exact same way?

### Client Onboarding
- **Best opening questions:** What are the first 3 things that happen when a client signs a contract?
- **Process questions:** Who triggers the onboarding sequence?
- **Data questions:** What information do you need from the client to start work?
- **Tool questions:** What tools do you use to collect their onboarding info?
- **Risk questions:** What goes wrong if onboarding is delayed?
- **Human-review questions:** Do you want to review the welcome packet before it is sent?
- **Success metric questions:** What defines a successful onboarding?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do you have a standard intake form, or is it just an email request?

### Document Collection
- **Best opening questions:** How much time do you spend chasing clients for missing files?
- **Process questions:** How do you track what documents are still missing?
- **Data questions:** Are the document names standardized?
- **Tool questions:** Do you use secure portals or just email attachments?
- **Risk questions:** Are these documents sensitive (e.g., tax, legal, medical, financial)?
- **Human-review questions:** Should a human verify the document is correct before stopping the reminders?
- **Success metric questions:** How fast do we want documents returned?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do we have a strict checklist of required documents?

### Invoicing
- **Best opening questions:** How often are invoices sent out late?
- **Process questions:** What triggers an invoice to be generated?
- **Data questions:** Are line items standard or completely custom?
- **Tool questions:** Which accounting tool do you use?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if the amount is wrong? (High financial risk)
- **Human-review questions:** Do you review all invoices before they are sent?
- **Success metric questions:** How much faster do we want to get paid?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Is your accounting software properly synced with your CRM?

### Payment Reminders
- **Best opening questions:** How do you track late payments?
- **Process questions:** What is the trigger for the first reminder?
- **Data questions:** Is the due date reliably tracked?
- **Tool questions:** Does your accounting tool already do this?
- **Risk questions:** Could we accidentally remind someone who already paid?
- **Human-review questions:** Should a human review the list of overdue accounts before sending emails?
- **Success metric questions:** What percentage of accounts are past due?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do you log payments the same day they arrive?

### Customer Support
- **Best opening questions:** What are the top 3 questions you get asked every week?
- **Process questions:** How do you decide who answers a ticket?
- **Data questions:** Do you have an internal knowledge base?
- **Tool questions:** Do you use a helpdesk or just a shared inbox?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if we give a customer the wrong answer?
- **Human-review questions:** Should the AI draft the response for you to approve, or reply instantly?
- **Success metric questions:** What is your target resolution time?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Are your current FAQs actually documented anywhere?

### Internal Operations
- **Best opening questions:** What is the most annoying internal task your team does?
- **Process questions:** What is the step-by-step process?
- **Data questions:** Where does the data come from?
- **Tool questions:** What tools are used internally?
- **Risk questions:** What breaks if this isn't done?
- **Human-review questions:** Does anyone need to approve this before it moves to the next step?
- **Success metric questions:** How much time will this save?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Is there a clear owner for this task?

### Reporting
- **Best opening questions:** What report takes you the longest to build every week?
- **Process questions:** When does it need to be delivered?
- **Data questions:** Is the data in one system, or do you have to pull it from three different places?
- **Tool questions:** Do you use spreadsheets or a BI tool?
- **Risk questions:** Are these reports shown to investors or clients?
- **Human-review questions:** Do you review the numbers before sending the report out?
- **Success metric questions:** Does this report actually drive business decisions?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Does the data need to be cleaned before the report is useful?

### CRM Updates
- **Best opening questions:** How often does your team forget to update the CRM?
- **Process questions:** What is the trigger to update a record (e.g., meeting ended)?
- **Data questions:** What specific fields are required?
- **Tool questions:** Which CRM are you using?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if the data is wrong?
- **Human-review questions:** Should a manager review pipeline changes?
- **Success metric questions:** What percentage of deals have complete data?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Are there too many required fields right now?

### Proposal Drafting
- **Best opening questions:** How much time do you spend writing a proposal from scratch?
- **Process questions:** What starts the proposal process?
- **Data questions:** Where does the pricing data come from?
- **Tool questions:** Do you use Word, Google Docs, or a proposal software?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if the pricing is calculated incorrectly?
- **Human-review questions:** Do you require manager approval before sending?
- **Success metric questions:** How fast do you want proposals out the door?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Do you have standard proposal templates?

### Weekly Finance Summaries
- **Best opening questions:** How long does it take to pull your weekly numbers?
- **Process questions:** Are you manually downloading CSVs?
- **Data questions:** Are bank feeds synced?
- **Tool questions:** Which accounting tool and reporting tool?
- **Risk questions:** Who has access to this data?
- **Human-review questions:** Require a human to review the summary before it goes to the wider team?
- **Success metric questions:** What decisions do you make based on this?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Are your books closed on time?

### Appointment Reminders
- **Best opening questions:** How often do people no-show?
- **Process questions:** When should the reminder go out?
- **Data questions:** Do you have correct phone numbers?
- **Tool questions:** Does your calendar tool already do this natively?
- **Risk questions:** What happens if the reminder has the wrong time?
- **Human-review questions:** None usually needed.
- **Success metric questions:** What is your target no-show rate?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Are appointments consistently logged in the same calendar?

### FAQ Response Drafting
- **Best opening questions:** How much time is spent answering simple questions?
- **Process questions:** Where do these questions come in (Email, Social, Chat)?
- **Data questions:** Is there a clear source of truth for the answers?
- **Tool questions:** What inbox handles this?
- **Risk questions:** What if the answer implies a guarantee we don't offer?
- **Human-review questions:** Should the AI draft the response for you to approve, or reply instantly? (Recommend Draft-only for pilot).
- **Success metric questions:** How many hours saved per week?
- **Cleanup-before-automation questions:** Is your FAQ document up to date?

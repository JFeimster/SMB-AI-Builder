Why not every repetitive task should be automated first

Some tasks are repetitive but highly sensitive. Others are frequent but provide zero revenue impact. The matrix filters out the noise and highlights the safest, most impactful pilots.

Ranking factors:

  • Frequency
  • Time saved
  • Revenue impact
  • Customer experience impact
  • Risk
  • Data readiness
  • Tool readiness
  • Human review needs

Recommendation labels

Automate Now High impact, low risk, ready data.
Clean Up First High impact, but messy data or unclear rules.
Keep Human High risk or requires complex human judgment.
Revisit Later Low impact, not worth the setup effort right now.

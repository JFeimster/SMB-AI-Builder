What this GPT is

An anti-hype diagnostic tool. It audits your current process, scores its readiness for automation, and helps you design a safe pilot plan.

What it is not

It is not a magic bullet that writes code, directly integrates your tools, or provides professional legal/financial advice. It does not promise to "magically fix your business."

Why workflow-first matters

If you automate a bad process, you just make mistakes faster. Cleaning up the workflow before introducing AI can reduce rework, scope creep, and customer-experience risk.

Who it helps

SMB Owners

Identify safe first-steps for automation without buying expensive tools.

Agencies & Consultants

Run rigorous discovery and define clear scopes before client engagements.

Automation Builders

Get precise agent blueprints and constraints instead of vague requests.

Safety stance

We advocate for Human-in-the-Loop systems. AI should draft, format, and prep; humans should review, approve, and send. This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review.

Why client automation requests are usually underspecified

Clients ask for "an AI that does X" without knowing their own process. If you build on a broken process, the failure is blamed on you. Discovery is your defense.

How agencies can use it in discovery:

  • Run client SOPs through the auditor
  • Show the readiness score to justify cleanup work
  • Identify missing rules before quoting a price
  • Define clear pilot boundaries

Client deliverables & packaging ideas

Deliverables

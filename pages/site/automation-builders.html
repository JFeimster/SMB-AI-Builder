Why readiness scoring improves build scope

When you build on a process that lacks clear data inputs or defined edge cases, your automation breaks. Assessing readiness forces the client to define the rules before you build.

Inputs builders should collect:

  • Exact data sources
  • Required permissions
  • Expected outputs
  • Failure states and fallback routing
  • Human approval checkpoints

How the GPT creates better specs

From vague to defined

The GPT translates "I want an AI to reply to emails" into a specific agent blueprint detailing routing rules, draft states, and required context.

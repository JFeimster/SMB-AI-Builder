Connector Playbooks

Connector Playbooks for SMB Workflow Automation

Use these playbooks to understand how to correctly and safely implement connectors for your business workflows.

How to use a connector playbook

Review the playbooks below before hooking up tools. Each playbook defines the best use cases, risky anti-patterns, and the amount of human review required to deploy it effectively.

Connector Playbook Cards

Google Drive

Best for: Document storage and template generation.

Do not use for: Dynamic database operations.

Common workflows: Client folder creation, PDF archiving.

Human review required: Low.

Risk notes: Watch out for permissions creep.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Gmail

Best for: Drafting outbound messages and parsing simple inquiries.

Do not use for: Fully automated unreviewed replies.

Common workflows: Lead triage, support drafting.

Human review required: High.

Risk notes: Sending unreviewed emails can hurt reputation.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Google Calendar

Best for: Scheduling blocks and syncing events.

Do not use for: Overriding critical meetings.

Common workflows: Discovery call booking.

Human review required: Low.

Risk notes: Double-booking risks if poorly configured.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

GitHub

Best for: Code syncing, issue tracking, and PR automation.

Do not use for: Storing production secrets.

Common workflows: Repo-to-deployment.

Human review required: Medium.

Risk notes: Ensure branch protection rules are active.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Notion

Best for: Internal wikis, content calendars, and project tracking.

Do not use for: High-volume transactional databases.

Common workflows: Weekly reporting, task boards.

Human review required: Low.

Risk notes: API rate limits on heavy syncing.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Airtable

Best for: Relational data mapping and complex ops tracking.

Do not use for: Highly sensitive raw PII without field restrictions.

Common workflows: CRM management, content production.

Human review required: Medium.

Risk notes: Exposing full base schemas accidentally.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

HubSpot

Best for: Lead scoring and marketing automation.

Do not use for: Unreviewed massive email blasts via API.

Common workflows: Lead follow-up, deal stage syncing.

Human review required: Medium.

Risk notes: Data duplication if matching rules fail.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Vercel

Best for: Frontend deployment and serverless execution.

Do not use for: Heavy long-running background tasks.

Common workflows: Auto-deploy on merge, preview environments.

Human review required: Medium.

Risk notes: Accidental production pushes without QA.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

QuickBooks

Best for: Invoice generation and expense syncing.

Do not use for: Automated payments without approval.

Common workflows: Draft invoice creation.

Human review required: High.

Risk notes: Extremely high risk for automated financial actions.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

Semrush

Best for: SEO audits and keyword tracking.

Do not use for: Automated unreviewed content generation based solely on data.

Common workflows: Competitor analysis, rank tracking.

Human review required: Medium.

Risk notes: API credit exhaustion.

Starter prompt: Needs setup

How To Use These Skills With the GPT

  1. Pick a workflow: Identify a single operational process you want to improve.
  2. Pick the skill: Choose the appropriate capability, such as an audit or blueprint builder.
  3. Give the GPT context: Provide the SMB AI Builder GPT with the specifics of your operation.
  4. Get the draft plan: Receive a customized, structured output using the chosen skill.
  5. Review risk: Critically assess any potential failure points or exceptions.
  6. Pilot safely: Test the implementation on a small, controlled scale first.

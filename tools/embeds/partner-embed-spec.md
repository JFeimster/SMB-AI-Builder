# Partner Embed Spec

## Partner Embed Wireframe

The partner embed wireframe must include the following components:

- **Header badge:** A badge identifying the widget.
- **Mini hero:** A short introductory hero section.
- **3-question readiness picker:** An interactive section with exactly 3 questions to assess workflow automation readiness.
- **Result preview:** An area to show a preview of the user's result based on the picker.
- **What the full GPT gives you:** A brief explanation of the value provided by the full SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder GPT.
- **CTA:** A clear call to action button linking to the Primary CTA URL.
- **Partner attribution:** A section acknowledging the partner providing the embed.
- **Disclaimer:** The required explicit disclaimer: 'This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review.'

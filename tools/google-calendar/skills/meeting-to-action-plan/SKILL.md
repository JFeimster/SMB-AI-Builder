# Skill: Meeting to Action Plan

## Purpose

Turn meeting context into clear tasks, owners, deadlines, follow-up drafts, and human-reviewed next steps.

## Use When

Use this skill when a workflow starts with a meeting, call, consultation, kickoff, sales appointment, or internal review.

## Inputs Needed

- Meeting purpose
- Attendees/roles
- Notes or agenda
- Decisions made
- Open questions
- Follow-up requirements
- Sensitive topics

## Outputs

- Action plan
- Task list
- Owners
- Follow-up drafts
- Open questions
- Review checkpoints

## Procedure

1. Identify meeting objective.
2. Extract decisions and open questions.
3. Convert next steps into tasks.
4. Assign owners where known.
5. Draft follow-up message if appropriate.
6. Flag sensitive or uncertain items for human review.

## Safety / Human Review

Do not invent decisions, commitments, deadlines, or attendee agreement. Sensitive customer, financial, legal, HR, lending, or compliance items require human confirmation.

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Tasks are specific.
- [ ] Owners are explicit or marked unknown.
- [ ] Dates are not invented.
- [ ] Follow-ups are draft-first.
- [ ] Sensitive issues are flagged.

## Example Prompt

```text
Turn this meeting context into an action plan. Identify decisions, tasks, owners, open questions, follow-up draft, and human review checkpoints.
```

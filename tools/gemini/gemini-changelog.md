## [1.0.0] - Initial Creation
### Added
- `README.md` defining scope and use cases.
- `gemini-agent-profile.md` defining strategic, SEO, UX, and safety roles.
- `gemini-project-context.md` centralizing product summary, URLs, and brand tone.
- `gemini-prompt-library.md` establishing standard prompts for code, copy, and SEO review.
- `gemini-research-rules.md` documenting strict citation and anti-hallucination rules.
- `gemini-code-review-rules.md` creating checklists for HTML, CSS, JS, and a11y.
- `gemini-copy-rules.md` establishing the anti-hype and operator-focused tone constraints.
- `gemini-seo-rules.md` defining strict structural, meta, and schema constraints.
- `gemini-multimodal-review-rules.md` creating guidelines for screenshot and visual design critique.
- `gemini-qa-checklist.md` adding a final verification checklist for Gemini outputs.
- `gemini-output-formats.md` standardizing Markdown structures for Gemini responses.
- `gemini-changelog.md` to track future updates to these tool operations.

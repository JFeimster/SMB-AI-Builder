# Brand Principles

The SMB AI Builder brand is not your typical soft, muted, B2B SaaS. It is designed to be loud, functional, and deeply practical.

## Core Principles

1.  **Loud but useful**
    *   The visual volume is turned up to 11, but never at the expense of utility. Information must be clear, readable, and actionable.

2.  **Anti-corporate SaaS**
    *   Avoid generic illustrations, vague buzzwords, soft pastels, and "samey" corporate aesthetics. The vibe is gritty, real, and slightly rebellious.

3.  **High contrast**
    *   Use stark contrasts (e.g., Ink Black against Acid Lime or Off White). This ensures legibility and creates a bold, neobrutalist impact.

4.  **Operator-friendly**
    *   Speak directly to the person doing the work. Use clear checklists, explicit labels, and straightforward language. Avoid fluffy theory.

5.  **Safety-aware**
    *   Design must communicate risk and caution where necessary. Use warning colors (Hazard Yellow, Signal Orange, Cyber Pink) for critical decisions or sensitive workflows.

6.  **Conversion-focused**
    *   CTAs should be impossible to miss. Use sticky elements, high-contrast buttons, and clear value propositions to drive action.

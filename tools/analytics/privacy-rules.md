# Privacy Rules

The analytics layer is strictly governed by the following privacy rules to ensure user and partner data protection.

## Core Rules

- **Do not track sensitive workflow contents**: Any details regarding the user's workflow processes, logic, or proprietary methods must not be collected.
- **Do not track uploaded documents**: No information or metadata about uploaded documents should be sent to analytics services.
- **Do not track private business details**: Specific business data (e.g., financials, employee counts, private IDs) must not be tracked.
- **Do not store personal data in URLs**: PII (Personally Identifiable Information) must never be passed through URL parameters or query strings.
- **Do not expose private partner data**: Ensure partner metrics and performance data remain confidential and are not exposed publicly or to unauthorized parties.

# Analytics Dashboard Plan

This document outlines the suggested metrics for an analytics dashboard tailored to the SMB AI Builder site.

## Suggested Metrics

- **CTA clicks**: Total volume and conversion rates for primary and secondary CTAs across the site.
- **Partner embed clicks**: Engagement rates on CTAs loaded specifically within partner embeds.
- **SEO page CTA rates**: Performance and conversion metrics for CTAs located on targeted SEO landing pages.
- **FAQ engagement**: Metrics tracking which FAQs are opened most frequently, identifying user pain points or areas of interest.
- **Resource clicks**: Tracking outbound clicks to Notion resources, GitHub repositories, or partner content.

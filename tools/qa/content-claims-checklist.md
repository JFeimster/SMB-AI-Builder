# Content Claims Checklist

This is a strict checklist. A failure here means the page cannot be published. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for hype, fake proof, and unlicensed advice.

## Hard Claim Checks
- [ ] No fabricated testimonials, illustrative testimonials, fake customer quotes, fake reviews, fake ratings, fake case studies, fake logos, fake partner claims, fake benchmarks, or invented proof are present. There are no exceptions.
- [ ] **No fake reviews**: No fabricated review stars, scores, or text.
- [ ] **No fake ratings**: No invented metrics (e.g., "Rated 4.9/5 by 10,000 users" if untrue).
- [ ] **No fake logos**: No "Trusted By" logo farms using brands that are not actual clients/partners.
- [ ] **No fake guarantees**: No promises of specific ROI, guaranteed time saved, or guaranteed revenue generated.
- [ ] **No compliance claims**: Do not claim the tool makes a workflow HIPAA, SOC2, or GDPR compliant on its own.
- [ ] **No professional advice claims**: Do not claim the tool replaces a lawyer, CPA, or licensed professional.

## Tone & Terminology Checks
- [ ] **No generic AI buzzwords**: Verify the copy does NOT use phrases like:
  - "unlock your potential"
  - "revolutionize your workflow"
  - "seamless experience"
  - "game-changing"
  - "cutting-edge"
  - "transform your business"
  - "leverage AI"
- [ ] **Direct Language**: Tone is operator-friendly, direct, and anti-hype.

## Sensitive Workflow Checks
- [ ] If a page, prompt, template, schema, embed, or document touches sensitive workflow categories (money, finance, billing, payment, private data, sensitive personal data, customer communication, document collection, legal, HR, medical, lending, underwriting, accounting, tax, compliance, client onboarding, customer support, refunds, pricing, business-critical decisions, or any workflow where an incorrect automated action could create customer, financial, legal, operational, or reputational risk), the full sensitive-workflow disclaimer appears visibly near the relevant safety, human review, or CTA section.
  > "This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review."
- [ ] **Safeguards Recommended**: For sensitive workflows, confirm the page recommends safe modes (e.g., "Draft-only AI assist", "Human approval before action").

# Skill: Follow-Up Draft Builder

## Purpose
Creates automated follow-up email sequences and templates.

## Use When
When planning sales follow-ups, client onboarding emails, or abandoned cart sequences.

## Inputs Needed
- Context of the follow-up (e.g., post-meeting, no response)
- Audience persona
- Key call to action (CTA)
- Timing/delays between emails

## Outputs
- A sequence of email drafts
- Recommended timing for each send
- Trigger conditions for the sequence

## Procedure
1. Determine the goal of the follow-up sequence.
2. Outline the steps and timing (e.g., Day 1, Day 3, Day 7).
3. Draft the email copy for each step, keeping it concise and operator-focused.
4. Define the trigger that starts the sequence and the condition that stops it.
5. Review against safety and tone rules.

## Safety / Human Review
- Do not pretend to send the emails.
- Ensure the tone is professional and not spammy.
- Include placeholders for personalization.

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Sequence has a clear goal and CTA?
- [ ] Timing is reasonable?
- [ ] Placeholders are clearly marked?

## Example Prompt
```
Can you help me draft a 3-step follow-up email sequence for leads who requested a quote but haven't replied to my initial proposal?
```

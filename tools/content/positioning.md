# Positioning

## Core Promise
We help you figure out exactly what to automate, what to clean up, and what to leave alone—before you waste time or break your business.

## Audience
Micro-SMB owners, agency owners, lean teams, freelancers, AI automation builders, consultants, and creator-operators who want to use AI but are overwhelmed by hype and risk.

## Pain Point
Automating a broken process just makes it fail faster. Businesses are jumping into AI without knowing if their data, process, or team is ready, leading to wasted money, broken tools, and frustrated customers.

## Mechanism
The Readiness Scoring Model. A 0-3 scale across 10 categories that provides a definitive score (0-30) telling you exactly where you stand.

## Offer
A free, comprehensive workflow audit and agent pilot plan built specifically for your exact situation, delivered via our custom GPT.

## Strategic Angle
We position against the hype. While others promise to "transform your business" with "seamless AI," we tell you to slow down, audit the mess, and build a safe pilot first. We are the adult in the room.

## CTA Strategy
Drive users directly to the SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder GPT to run an audit. Emphasize "scoring" and "building a plan" over "buying a tool."

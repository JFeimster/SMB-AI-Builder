# Audience Personas

## Micro-SMB owners
They have limited time, messy data, and are overwhelmed by AI hype. They need practical, safe, and immediate solutions to save time without breaking their existing processes.

## Agency owners
They are looking for scalable ways to audit client workflows and propose AI solutions safely. They need structured plans to avoid scope creep and manage client expectations.

## AI automation builders
They want to pre-qualify clients and workflows before they start writing code or building Zaps. The audit saves them from trying to automate impossible messes.

## Freelancers
Similar to Micro-SMBs, they need to save time on admin tasks but cannot afford mistakes that damage client relationships.

## Consultants
They use the audit as a diagnostic tool to provide strategic advice to their clients before recommending specific software purchases.

## Creator-operators
They run lean operations and need to automate the backend (emails, scheduling) safely so they can focus on content creation.

## Lean teams
Small internal teams trying to punch above their weight. They need clear guidance on what is safe to automate so they don't break the customer experience.

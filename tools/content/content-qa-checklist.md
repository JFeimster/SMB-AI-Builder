# Content QA Checklist

Before publishing any new page or content update, verify the following:

- [ ] **Voice & Tone:** Is it direct, punchy, practical, and anti-hype?
- [ ] **Forbidden Phrases:** Run a search for forbidden phrases (e.g., "unlock your potential", "leverage AI").
- [ ] **Approved Language:** Are we using approved framing ("Stop automating broken workflows")?
- [ ] **CTAs:** Are the CTAs correct and linking to the primary GPT URL?
- [ ] **Safety & Disclaimers:** Is the general disclaimer present and visible near safety/CTA sections? Are specific disclaimers used for sensitive workflows?
- [ ] **Formatting:** Are H1s and H2s used correctly? Is the text easily skimmable?
- [ ] **Links:** Do all internal and external links work correctly?
- [ ] **SEO/Metadata:** (If applicable) Are the title, meta description, and schema valid and unique?

# Voice and Tone

The voice of the SMB AI Builder content must strictly adhere to the following principles:

- **Direct:** Cut the fluff. Get straight to the point.
- **Punchy:** Short sentences. Active voice.
- **Practical:** Focus on execution, steps, and real-world results.
- **No-BS:** Speak like an operator, not a marketer.
- **SMB-friendly:** Acknowledge the realities of small businesses (messy data, limited time).
- **Safety-aware:** Emphasize risk, human-in-the-loop, and testing.
- **Anti-hype:** Refuse vague promises. We talk about audits, scores, and safe pilots, not "revolutions" or "magic."

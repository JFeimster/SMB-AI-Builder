# FAQ Library

Use these standard FAQs on pages.

**Is this just another AI automation chatbot?**
No. This is a planning and readiness layer. It audits your current process, scores its readiness, and builds a safe pilot plan *before* you start trying to connect tools.

**What workflow should I start with?**
Start with something internal, low-risk, and repetitive. Lead follow-up summaries, document collection, or internal reporting are great first choices.

**What if my process is messy?**
That is exactly why you audit it first. The GPT will tell you if your process is too messy to automate and give you a checklist of what to clean up before you try.

**Will this build the automation for me?**
No. It builds the *blueprint*. You take the plan it generates and build the automation yourself (or hand it to a developer) using Zapier, Make, or custom code.

**Does it connect to my tools?**
The GPT itself does not connect to your live data. It is a standalone strategy tool. The pilot plan it generates will recommend how to connect your tools safely.

**Is this safe for customer-facing workflows?**
Customer-facing workflows require high readiness scores. The GPT will likely recommend "draft-only mode" or "human approval before action" for anything touching your customers.

**Can agencies use this with clients?**
Yes. Agencies can use this to audit client workflows and present a clear, scoped pilot plan before beginning development.

**What do I get after using it?**
You get a specific readiness score (0-30), a breakdown of risks, and a concrete, step-by-step pilot plan.

**Is this legal, financial, tax, HR, or compliance advice?**
No. This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review.

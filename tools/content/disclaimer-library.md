# Disclaimer Library

Ensure the appropriate disclaimer is visibly placed near safety or CTA sections for relevant content.

## General Disclaimer (Must be included on sensitive workflows)
This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review.

## Customer-facing Workflows
AI outputs touching customers directly should always begin in draft-only mode or require human approval before sending. Monitor for hallucinations and brand voice deviations.

## Finance Workflows
Never automate money movement or final financial reporting without rigorous human review and audit logs. Start by automating data extraction and summarization only.

## Legal, Tax, & Compliance Workflows
AI should not generate final legal or tax documents. Use AI to organize inputs, draft summaries, and flag missing information for a qualified professional to review.

## HR Workflows
Hiring, firing, and employee evaluation decisions must be made by humans. AI can assist with organizing resumes or drafting standard communications, but must not be the final decision-maker.

## Partner Embeds
This tool is provided for educational and planning purposes. Integration with third-party tools is done at your own risk.

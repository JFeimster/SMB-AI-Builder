# Jules Accessibility Checklist

## Structure
- Page has semantic landmarks.
- Page has one H1.

## Keyboard Navigation
- Can tab through page.
- Skip link exists where consistent with the site.

## Focus States
- Visible focus rings.

## Links and Buttons
- Buttons are buttons.
- Links are links.
- Interactive elements have accessible names.
- CTA links are descriptive.

## Forms
- Forms have labels.

## Images and Media
- Images have alt text.
- Decorative visuals are hidden where needed.
- No text trapped in images.

## Color and Contrast
- Color contrast is strong.

## Motion
- Motion is not required to understand content.

## FAQ and Accordion Components
- Accordions expose state.

## Final Accessibility QA
- Verify all checklist items before deployment.

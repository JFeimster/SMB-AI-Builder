# Jules SEO Rules

## Page Requirements
Every SEO page needs:
- Unique title
- Unique meta description
- One H1
- Descriptive H2s
- Internal links
- CTA band
- FAQ section
- JSON-LD where useful

## Metadata Rules
- Titles and meta descriptions must be unique.
- Do not use fake ratings or reviews.

## Search Intent Rules
- Search-intent alignment
- Helpful content that answers a real question
- No copied boilerplate pages

## Internal Linking Rules
- Pages should contain descriptive internal links.

## FAQ Rules
- Answer practical, real questions without fluff.

## Schema Rules
- JSON-LD where useful
- No fake review schema

## Prohibited SEO Tactics
- Thin doorway pages
- Keyword stuffing
- Fake reviews
- Fake aggregate ratings
- Fake testimonials
- Fake author credentials
- Fake claims
- Unsupported competitor comparisons
- No copied boilerplate pages

## Final SEO QA
- Verify all rules are met before finalizing a page.

# Privacy Terms Page Builder

## Purpose
Create privacy, terms, and disclaimer pages that explain actual data handling and product limits.

## Use this skill when
- Drafting privacy or terms pages.
- Adding disclaimers for workflow planning, AI pilot design, or sensitive workflows.
- Reviewing claims about user data, cookies, analytics, forms, embeds, or external links.

## Source-of-truth files to read first
- `tools/jules/JULES.md`
- `tools/jules/jules.json`
- `tools/jules/copy-rules.md`
- `tools/jules/qa-checklist.md`

## Process
1. Identify what the repo actually collects, embeds, links to, or stores.
2. Write plain-language boundaries and disclaimers.
3. Avoid legal advice framing.
4. Flag missing implementation details instead of inventing them.

## Guardrails
- Do not pretend to be legal counsel.
- Do not invent compliance certifications, data practices, or regulatory coverage.
- Keep sensitive-workflow disclaimers visible and direct.

# SEO Refresh Log

This document tracks updates, refreshes, and audits of existing SEO content to ensure freshness and accuracy.

## Refresh Strategy
- **Frequency:** High-traffic pages should be reviewed quarterly; others bi-annually.
- **Criteria for Refresh:**
  - Changes in underlying tool mechanics or recommendations.
  - Identification of new target keywords or search intent shifts.
  - Stale `<lastmod>` dates in the sitemap (older than 6 months).

## Update Types
- **Content Refresh:** Updating body text, FAQs, or adding new sections.
- **Metadata Fix:** Adjusting Title, Description, or Schema.
- **Link Audit:** Fixing broken internal links or adding new relevant links.
- **Performance:** Optimizing images or layout for core web vitals.

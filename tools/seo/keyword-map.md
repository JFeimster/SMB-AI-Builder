# Keyword Map

This document outlines the required keyword groups for the SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder project and maps them to content types.

## Primary Commercial Keywords
Mapped to: **Home, Product Pages, Core Tools**
- SMB AI workflow automation
- AI workflow audit
- AI automation readiness scorecard
- AI agent pilot plan
- Custom GPT for business automation

## Problem-Aware Keywords
Mapped to: **General SEO Pages, Guides, Blog/Resource Hub**
- what should I automate first in my business
- how to automate small business workflows
- how to use AI agents in a small business
- workflow automation mistakes
- automating broken processes

## Agency Keywords
Mapped to: **Agency-focused Pages, Resource Templates, Checklists**
- AI automation discovery template
- client automation audit checklist
- AI agency workflow audit
- automation readiness assessment

## Safety Keywords
Mapped to: **Workflow Audit Pages, Trust & Safety Pages**
- human in the loop AI workflow
- AI automation guardrails
- safe AI agent pilot
- AI workflow rollback plan

## Mapping Strategy
- Use these keywords naturally in headers (H1, H2) and body content.
- Never keyword stuff.
- Address the user's search intent clearly with practical, actionable advice.

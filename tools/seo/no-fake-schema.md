# Hard Rules: No Fake Schema & No Hype

To maintain absolute trust and authority, and to avoid search engine penalties, we adhere to strict ethical SEO guidelines. There are zero exceptions to these rules.

## The "No Fake" Directives

- **No fake reviews:** Never create dummy `Review` schema or textual testimonials.
- **No fake ratings:** Never invent an `aggregateRating` (e.g., "4.9 stars out of 500 reviews") to get star rich snippets.
- **No fake offers:** Do not use `Offer` schema to list fake prices or "free" tags if not genuinely applicable to a measurable product.
- **No fake awards:** Never claim industry awards we have not verifiably won.
- **No fake case studies:** Never invent client names, metrics, or success stories to fill out content templates.

## The "No Hype" Directives (Banned Words)

Our tone is direct, operator-friendly, and practical. We avoid vague AI buzzwords.

**Do NOT use the following phrases:**
- "unlock your potential"
- "revolutionize your workflow"
- "seamless experience"
- "game-changing"
- "cutting-edge"
- "transform your business"
- "leverage AI"

## Enforcement
Any pull request containing fake schema, invented metrics, or banned hype words must be rejected immediately during the QA phase.

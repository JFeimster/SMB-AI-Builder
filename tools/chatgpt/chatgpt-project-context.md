# Project Context: SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder

Use this document to ground any generation, revision, or planning tasks in the reality of the product.

## 1. Product Summary
SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder helps small business owners, operators, freelancers, agencies, consultants, and automation builders audit workflows, score automation readiness, identify automation opportunities, design AI-powered workflows, and build safer AI agent pilot plans *before* buying tools, hiring developers, or automating broken processes.

## 2. Audience
- **Primary:** Small Business Owners, COOs, Operations Managers, Solopreneurs, Freelancers.
- **Secondary:** Automation Agencies, Consultants, Fractional Executives, Low-Code/No-Code Developers.
- **Mindset:** Pragmatic, time-starved, cautious about AI hype, looking for ROI, worried about breaking existing processes or compromising data security.

## 3. The Core Offer
We offer a structured, strategic planning layer. We do not build the final software. We provide the *blueprint* and the *audit* to ensure the user is actually ready to build.

### Key Value Propositions
- Stop guessing what to automate.
- Score your readiness before spending money.
- Identify risks in sensitive workflows (money, compliance, customer-facing).
- Build a safe, constrained pilot plan.

## 5. Tone & Voice
- **Direct & Practical:** Get straight to the point. No fluff. Use bullet points and checklists.
- **Anti-Hype:** Avoid tech-utopian language. Focus on brass tacks.
- **Operator-Friendly:** Speak like an experienced operations manager talking to a peer.

## 6. Safety Stance
We take workflow safety extremely seriously.
- **Strict Rule:** We do not provide legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice.
- **Sensitive Workflows:** Any workflow touching money, customer communication, or compliance data must heavily index on "Human-in-the-loop" (HITL), Draft-Only modes, and Exception Review.

## 7. Visual Style (For Code/Design Generation)
- **Aesthetic:** Neobrutalism + Futurist Fintech + Cyberpunk.
- **Characteristics:**
  - High contrast (Ink black backgrounds).
  - Thick, harsh borders on UI elements.
  - Hard, offset drop shadows (no soft blurs).
  - Vivid, neon accent colors.
  - Monospace or structured sans-serif typography for data/code segments.

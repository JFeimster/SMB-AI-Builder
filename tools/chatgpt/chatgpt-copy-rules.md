# Copywriting Rules

Follow these rules for all text generated for the SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder project.

## 1. Voice & Tone
- **Direct & Punchy:** Get straight to the point. Respect the reader's time.
- **SMB-Friendly:** Speak to operations managers, founders, and consultants. Use language they use on the shop floor or in the back office.
- **Operator-Focused:** Emphasize the reality of running a business—messy data, edge cases, and the risk of breaking things.
- **Authoritative but Humble:** We are experts in planning and readiness, but we acknowledge that AI is not magic.

## 2. The Anti-Hype Mandate
Never use tech-utopian fluff or vague AI buzzwords.

**Banned Words / Phrases:**
- "Unlock your potential"
- "Revolutionize your workflow"
- "Seamless experience"
- "Game-changing"
- "Cutting-edge"
- "Transform your business"
- "Leverage AI" (use "use AI" or "build with AI")
- "Magic"

**Approved Alternatives:**
- Audit, Score, Map, Build, Deploy, Test, Plan, Review, Mitigate.

## 3. Outcome-Focused Framing
Instead of selling "AI," sell the outcome of *planning for AI*.
- **Weak:** "Our tool uses advanced AI to revolutionize how you work."
- **Strong:** "Audit your manual processes before you spend money on AI tools."
- **Weak:** "Get a seamless automated experience."
- **Strong:** "Build a safe, draft-only pilot plan that keeps humans in the loop."

## 4. Safety & Realism First
Always position the product as a risk-mitigation tool.
- Acknowledge that automating bad processes just creates faster bad processes.
- For any mention of money, compliance, or customer service, emphasize "Human Approval Before Action" and "Draft-Only Mode."

## 5. Structural Guidelines
- **Use Active Voice:** "The GPT audits your workflow," not "Your workflow is audited by the GPT."
- **Short Sentences:** Keep it readable. Break up walls of text.
- **Checklists & Bullets:** Operators love checklists. Use them heavily for readiness factors and audit steps.
- **Clear CTAs:** Call-to-action buttons must be unambiguous verbs: "Audit My First Workflow," "Score My Readiness."

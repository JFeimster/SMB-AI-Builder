# Prompt: Conversion Copy Pass

**Purpose:** Prompt for sharpening conversion copy.

## Pre-requisites
Read `JULES.md`, `jules.json`, and `jules.style.md` as source-of-truth files before starting.

## Instructions
Review the copy on the designated files and sharpen the messaging to match the product's core positioning. The tone should be direct, no-BS, operator-friendly, anti-hype, practical, and safety-aware.

### Remove
Identify and remove the following language and concepts:
- "unlock your potential"
- "revolutionize"
- "seamless"
- "cutting-edge"
- "game-changing"
- "transform your business"
- "leverage AI"
- Generic AI assistant language
- Vague enterprise SaaS language
- Fake urgency
- Fake proof (e.g., fake testimonials, reviews, metrics, unsupported claims)

### Replace With
Replace vague or hype-driven copy with these core messages:
- "Stop automating broken workflows."
- "Audit the mess first."
- "Score the workflow before AI touches it."
- "Know what to automate, clean up, or keep human."
- "Build a pilot plan your business can survive."
- "Plan the workflow before choosing the tool."

## Copy Quality Checks
Ensure the following elements meet the tone guidelines:
- [ ] **Hero Clarity Checks:** Is the main headline direct and problem-focused? Does it avoid hype words?
- [ ] **CTA Clarity Checks:** Are the calls to action action-oriented and clear (e.g., "Audit My First Workflow", "Score My Automation Readiness")?
- [ ] **Safety Stance Checks:** Is the copy realistic about AI risks and the need for human-in-the-loop oversight?
- [ ] **Anti-Hype Checks:** Have all instances of "seamless", "revolutionize", etc., been removed?
- [ ] **No Fake Proof Checks:** Has all fabricated social proof, metrics, or guarantees been removed?
- [ ] **Operator-Friendly Language:** Does the copy sound like it's written for a pragmatic small business owner or operator?

## Output Format
After completing the copy pass, provide a final summary formatted as follows:
1. **Key Copy Changes:** Highlights of the major copy updates made.
2. **Removed Hype Language:** A list of specific hype words or fake claims that were purged.
3. **Files Updated:** A list of the files that were modified.

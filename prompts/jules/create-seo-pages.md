## File Path Rules
- From `pages/seo/*.html`, link CSS as `../../styles.css`.
- From `pages/seo/*.html`, link JS as `../../script.js` with `defer`.

## Content & Layout Rules
- **Helpful Content:** Follow search intent rules and write genuinely helpful content.
- **No thin pages:** Avoid thin doorway pages and copied boilerplate.
- **Structure:**
  - One exactly matching H1 per page.
  - Unique title and meta description per page.
  - Include Article JSON-LD and FAQPage JSON-LD where appropriate.
  - Include 4 to 6 FAQs per page.
  - Include internal links.
  - Include a CTA band near the end of the page.

## Anti-Hype & Content Rules
- Tone: Direct, no-BS, operator-friendly, anti-hype, practical, safety-aware.
- **Approved language examples:** "Stop automating broken workflows.", "Audit the mess first.", "Score the workflow before AI touches it.", "Build a pilot plan your business can actually survive."
- **Banned language:** "unlock your potential", "revolutionize your workflow", "seamless experience", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "transform your business", "leverage AI", "AI-powered magic", "effortless automation", "fully autonomous business".
- **No fake proof:** Do not invent or imply fake testimonials, ratings, review schema, reviews, logos, case studies, guarantees, compliance claims, partner relationships, certifications, benchmarks, integrations, or unsupported competitor claims.

## Safety Disclaimer Rule
Where relevant, include the following safety disclaimer:
> This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review.

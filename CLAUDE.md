# Claude Tool-Ops Layer

## Project Overview
**SMB AI Workflow & Agent Builder**
This project helps small business owners, operators, freelancers, agencies, and lean teams audit business workflows, identify automation opportunities, design practical AI-powered automations, and build safe AI agent plans before investing in tools or development work.

## Folder Map
- `/app/`: Next.js App Router endpoints and demos.
- `/docs/`: Knowledge files, action catalogs, examples, architecture notes.
- `/tools/claude/`: Claude-specific tool-ops layer for drafting, reviewing, and editing.
- `/pages/seo/`: SEO landing pages and comparison pages.

## Preferred Editing Behavior
- Be a strategic reviewer, long-form drafter, code reviewer, SEO critic, and safety copy checker.
- Focus on practical, direct, operator-friendly language with checklists and placeholders.
- Avoid fluffy deployment theory or vague AI buzzwords.

## Copy Voice
- Direct, anti-hype, workflow-first positioning.
- Prefer useful detail over vague AI copy.
- Keep claims honest and specific.
- Avoid phrases like "unlock your potential", "revolutionize your workflow", "seamless experience", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "transform your business", and "leverage AI".

## Design System
- Neobrutalist, futurist fintech, and cyberpunk aesthetic.
- High contrast, thick borders, hard offset shadows, ink black backgrounds, and vivid accent colors.
- HTML/CSS is plain semantic HTML, mobile-first layouts, exactly one H1 per page.

## SEO Standards
- Unique titles and meta descriptions, descriptive H2s.
- Valid JSON-LD schema (Article and FAQPage).
- Accessible CTA links, visible focus states.
- SEO pages must include a consistent header/nav/footer, 4-6 FAQs per page, and a CTA band near the end.

## Safety / Disclaimer Rules
- Never commit or include secrets, API keys, private tokens, or real environment variables.
- Do not frame GPT output as legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, or compliance advice.
- For sensitive workflows, include the exact disclaimer visibly: "This GPT supports workflow planning and pilot design. It is not legal, financial, tax, HR, lending, accounting, medical, underwriting, or compliance advice. Sensitive decisions should stay under qualified human review."

## QA Checklist
- Are all CTA links using the correct Primary CTA URL?
- Are there no invented testimonials, ratings, logos, revenue claims, compliance claims, case studies, or guarantees?
- Are safety disclaimers included where necessary?
- Does the copy reflect the direct, no-BS voice?
- Is the HTML semantic and accessible?
